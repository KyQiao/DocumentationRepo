Rules of using hpc3

x-gpu 5 2 x Intel Xeon Silver 4210 (2*10-core) 256GB RTX6000
x-gpu 3 2 x Intel Xeon Silver 4210 (2*10-core) 512GB RTX6000

We can use 4 x-gpu node.

View your job

Purpose Command
To check what queues (partitions) are available: sinfo
To submit job: sbatch
To view the queue status: squeue
To view the queue status of your job: squeue -u $USER
To cancel a running or pending job: scancel
To view detailed information of your job: scontrol show job
